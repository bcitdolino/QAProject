About Us

Ligaya 甜美 is a local cafe that offers a fusion of Hong Kong and Filipino desserts. Our goal is to establish a dessert cafe that combines two different cultures. Customers can enjoy their sweets in our modern café, which has been designed to have a nostalgic feel. Our purpose is to bring you authentic desserts and fulfil your sweet taste at the same time.

Check out our exquisite desserts prepared from authentic, traditional ingredients from both the Filipino and Hong Kong cultures.
